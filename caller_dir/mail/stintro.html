How we work

 

Before we start with how we work , let me introduce you to "getRep" ( getIITians representative). A getRep will be assigned to each parent-teacher node and he/she will be single point of contact for all future queries starting with walk through of the process below -

 

Step 1  - Upon enquiry from student/parents, getRep will note your convenient time for scheduling first free demo class

Step 2  - On confirming teachers availability, getRep will fix first class as per mutually agreed time between student and teacher

Step 3 (a)  - On the day of demo class, getRep along with our faculty will visit your home or online ( as requested) for introduction.

While we understand that child's education is the prime focus of the parent's  life, getRep will address all your queries right from fees, guidelines, schedule to other details.

Step 3 (b) - getRep will share a google spreadsheet with parents, teacher and student for updating future class details, upcoming test date and revision reminders.

Step 3 (c) - While getRep is addressing your requirements,simulatneously our faculty will provide one hour free demo session to the student.  

Step 4- Parents will confirm the teacher to the getRep later and classes will commence thereafter as per the agreed timings.

Step 5 - Payments will be collected at the end of every month by either online transfer into getIITians account or cheque will be collected by getRep
