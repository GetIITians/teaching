About  getIITians

getIITians is India's leading platform which connects parents and teacher to provide quality education. We provide both home tuition and online classes. getIITians take pride in their faculty as all the tutors are

IITians who are either students/graduate/academic or working professional. We strongly understand tutors passion for teaching and enhancing skills of their student but we also understand that rewards increases passion.

Therefore at getIITians we charge premium for providing quality education and pass it on our teachers.

 

How we work

1.     Tutors have to register at www.getiitians.com

2.      getRep will connect to you within 72 hours to schedule short conversation at our office or online.

3.     Tutors will give demo class of 15 minutes to their respective getRep.

·         Home tutors will have to visit office for demo

·         Online tutors will give online demo *

4.     Tutors will sign Terms & condition form

5.     As soon as students send enquiry about the teacher, intimation message will be send to all the teacher in the nearby locality. Tutor who first accept the class will get the demo class.

6.     getRep will ask your and students suitable time and schedule first free demo class.

7.     On the day of demo class, getRep will go along with you to students home. getRep will cater to parents query while tutor will give their class

8.     If tutor get confirmed , we will share students number with teacher for scheduling future classes.

9.     Payments will be collected by getRep and will be transferred to tutors account after deducting getIITians commission of 25%.
